Founder

Andrew Burnie has a PhD in Computer Science, commercial management experience in insurance and at Hitachi Consulting, and an innate understanding of business needs. His background includes undergraduate Economics (Cambridge) and Management Studies (Judge Business School) as well as postgraduate Finance (Grenoble Graduate School of Business and CFA Levels I and II). He creates Artificial Intelligence (AI) products being a specialist in applying new Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to FinTech. Experienced in applying Machine Learning (neural networks, random forests and penalised regression) to customer analytics, analysing social media text and generating prediction models using Python, R and SQL. His work has been presented at ACM SIGIR, and published in Royal Society Open Science, Frontiers in Blockchain and Ledger.
